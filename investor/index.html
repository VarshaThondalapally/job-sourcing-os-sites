Category definition

This is not a job scraper.

Scraping answers what was posted. A sourcing operating system asks whether the role is live, reachable, aligned, safe to pursue, and likely to reach a human.

The problem

Job extraction creates motion. It does not create signal.

The failure mode is not that candidates cannot find postings. The failure mode is that they cannot tell which postings are alive, which person-route matters, and what proof will make a human respond.

What a scraper gives

  • Title, company, URL, source
  • Sometimes location and salary
  • Possible posting date
  • Unverified mirror data

What hiring actually needs

  • Active role proof
  • Person-proven human route
  • Blocker checks
  • Specific fit proof
  • Persona send/hold QC
  • Response monitoring
  • Feedback from outcomes

Human-in-the-loop controls

The system is AI-native, but it is not AI-authoritarian.

The most important control is knowing where AI must stop. The system treats AI as a research analyst and operations assistant, not as the final decision-maker.

AI can prepare

  • Gather and compare sources
  • Extract role facts and route hints
  • Draft fit arguments with evidence labels
  • Label human routes A/B/C/D with public evidence
  • Flag blockers and missing proof
  • Update trackers after confirmed actions

Human must approve

  • Final fit sentence
  • Person-route confidence and send/hold decision
  • Relocation and work-authorization stance
  • Sending email or submitting forms
  • Trial assignment offers
  • Sensitive or confidential claims

Evidence labels

Observed: directly seen in source.

Inferred: reasoned from multiple signals.

Unverified: plausible but not proven.

Blocked: cannot proceed without new evidence.

Stop command: company-level personalization is not person-level outreach.

Why now

AI made applying easier. That made human signal harder.

As AI increases application volume, differentiation shifts from polished documents to verified routes, specific proof, and learning from actual response data.

01

Application volume is cheap

Generic AI-generated applications are easier to produce and easier to ignore.

02

Posting truth is fragmented

LinkedIn, ATS mirrors, YC, company pages, and aggregators disagree.

03

Human routes matter more

Hidden CTAs, founder posts, team pages, and public person evidence often matter more than Apply buttons.

04

Feedback compounds

Replies, rejections, silence, and task requests become sourcing intelligence.
